About

Explore Berlin was founded in 2017 by three of our tour guides - Nels, Sara and Lola. They love the city and wanted to share their knowledge and passion with others. The goal was to allow visitors to dive deeper, discover the hidden paths and the hidden places, see the city through the eyes of the locals, the artists, the rebels.

We want to provide unique and personalized experience to each and every visitor. To enable people to not only explore the city, but experience it. We provide pre-planned tours, as well as personalized guides that will adapt to your own goals and requests. Reach out, tell us what you want to visit, and we'll organize it for you from start to finish.
