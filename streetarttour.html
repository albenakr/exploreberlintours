Tour Overview

What to Expect

So much of Berlin's history can be told through the Berlin Wall. Walk through the East Side Gallery and discover what was once a symbol of division being a canvas for messages of hope, peace and unity. We'll show you some of Berlin's most famous murals and you'll get a chance to meet one of the artists towards the end.

Tour Details

Tour duration: 4 hours.
Meeting point: Boxhagener Platz.
Start time: 2:30pm.
